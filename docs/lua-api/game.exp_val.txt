--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
--- The Dungeon Throne API Table: game.exp_val                               ---
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

---
get(id)
Returns the amount of experience <id> yields when killed.
---

---
set(id, exp)
Sets the amount of experience <id> yields when killed to <exp>.
---

---
increase(id, exp)
Increases the amount of experience <id> yields when killed by <exp>.
---

---
decrease(id, exp)
Decreases the amount of experience <id> yields when killed by <exp>.
---
