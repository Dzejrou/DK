--- The Dungeon Throne API Table: game.lls                                   ---

---
set_max_time(id, time)
Set the time to live of <id> to <time>.
---

---
get_max_time(id)
Returns the time to live of <id>.
---

---
get_curr_time(id)
Returns the current life time of <id>.
---

---
advance_curr_time(id, time)
Adds <time> second to the life timer of <id>.
